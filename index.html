    // OPERATOR BRIEFING

    Basics

    Classic rules: reveal every safe tile in the 12×12 grid (20 mines). Numbers show adjacent mines. Your first click is always safe. Left-click / tap reveals · right-click / long-press flags.

    Power tiles (2 per board — reveal to collect)

    Scanner Pulse — pick a revealed tile; safely reveals the plus-shape around it (range 2). Mines are never revealed.

    Shield — passive. Absorbs one mine click; the mine stays buried and the run continues.

    Defuser — probe one hidden tile. A mine is neutralized into a safe tile (numbers recalculate); a dud burns the charge.

    Corrupted tiles (4 per board, never mines)

    Revealing one is safe but triggers a glitch: visual static, +2s on the clock, or a 5s heatmap jam. Worth +25 score each.

    Heatmap

    Toggle with H. Tiles are tinted by estimated mine risk from neighboring numbers — hover (or long-press on mobile) for the percentage. It's a heuristic, not an oracle.

    Winning

    Clear the grid and complete both stabilization objectives to fully stabilize the reactor. Score: +10/safe tile, +25/corrupted, +50/objective, time bonus, and combo chains for fast consecutive reveals.
